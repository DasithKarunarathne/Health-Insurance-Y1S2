TERMS AND CONDITION

  1. Coverage Details

    Describes the medical services and treatments—such as hospital stays, doctor visits, prescription medication, etc.—that are covered under the policy.

  2. Premiums

    Indicates the total amount you must pay for insurance, the frequency of payments, and any grace periods for past-due amounts.

  3. Deductibles

    Indicates how much you have to pay out-of-pocket before your insurance starts to pay.

  4. Co-payments/Co-insurance

    Explains the fixed amount or percentage of medical expenses you are responsible for after meeting the deductible.

  5. Exclusions

    Contains a list of particular medical services, procedures, or ailments that the policy does not cover.

  6. Pre-existing Conditions

    Describes the handling of pre-existing conditions, including any waiting periods prior to the start of coverage.

  7. Network Providers

    Indicates whether you have the freedom to select healthcare providers outside of the network or if the insurance plan forces you to use doctors within a specific network in order to receive complete coverage.

  8. Claims Process

    Describes the steps involved in submitting a claim for payment as well as how long it will take to process one.
